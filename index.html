About

Hi! I am Qiyao, a final-year PhD in the Delft Institute of Applied Mathematics, Numerical Analysis group at Delft University of Technology in the Netherlands. My promoters are Prof. dr. ir. Fred Vermolen at Hasselt University, Belgium and Prof. Kees Vuik.

My work mainly focuses on mathematical modelling in biology, in particular, wound healing and the evolution of cell geometry. Besides, I am also interested in improving the accuracy of the solutions to the momentum equilibrium equations that contain the Dirac Delta distributions.
